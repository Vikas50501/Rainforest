Our Profile

Welcome to Rainforest Munnar Resort

The Rainforest Munnar Resort is ideally located in a deep-forest like environment, thickly surrounded by huge, mossy wild trees, ferns and thickets.

Popularly known as the High Ranges, Munnar is perhaps a perfect reflection of the Garden of Eden in Kerala. Away from the otherwise mundane hustle and bustle of a modern lifestyle, retire into the cozy lap of nature with some of the most endangered and protected wildlife around to amuse and surprise your family and friends.

Just 10 km from the bustling Munnar town, this is the perfect address for a memorable vacation. Surrounded by mist and natural green all year round, you will definitely feel like staying here forever.

The bungalow

Designed With You in Mind

The spacious bungalow has been intelligently laid out to make every moment of your stay comfortable — large bedrooms with attached bathrooms and balconies, and a single living-cum-dining area that easily accommodates large or small families.

All rooms have wooden flooring, a wooden ceiling and a private balcony facing the cardamom plantation. If you love nature, this is the ideal place to be.
