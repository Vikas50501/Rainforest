The Kashmir of the South

A Misty Village in the Western Ghats

Kanthalloor is a village known as the Southern Kashmir due to its chilly climate and misty atmosphere. Nestled in the Western Ghats of India, it is situated in Kerala around 50 kms west of Munnar. This is the only place in Kerala where the ambient temperature does not go beyond 19°C.

The salubrious climate, the picturesque landscapes, a wide variety of tropical crops coupled with the close proximity of Munnar, has transformed this village into a tourist destination — presenting the visitor with an excellent opportunity to encounter a diversity of scenery and become acquainted with rural life on the intimate scale of the hills.

The farm stay

A Two-Bedroom Country Farm House

At Rainforest Kanthalloor Orchard we offer a unique two-bedroom country farm house experience within an exclusive organic farm environment. To protect the special features we have crafted for genuine enthusiasts — and to prevent overuse — this facility is available in a limited and controlled manner during certain seasons.

Here we have recreated the farm-house concept — a replica of how our older generations lived in harmony with nature. This two-acre fertile land is abundant with fruit-bearing trees rare to be found elsewhere in India, along with exotic varieties of vegetables, herbs, fruiting and flowering plants developed from abroad.
