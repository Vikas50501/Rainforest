For the newly wedded

An Intimate Escape in the Trees

Complementing the romance of the location and enhancing the senses, the Honeymoon Wooden Cottage is a special and intimate space — built for the newly wedded to enjoy their stay in a house made completely of natural wood from pine trees, elevated 10 feet above the ground and facing a mesmerizing natural forest from all sides.

Your stay begins with a complimentary cake, karaoke music and a freshly cut flower arrangement. On request we also arrange a candle-light dinner, barbecue & campfire, spice-garden tours, mountain trekking and sight-seeing trips.

Plan Your Honeymoon Escape
Built the Canadian way

A Pine-Wood House, 10 Feet Above the Forest

Built on an expansive area of 500 square feet in complete natural pine wood and placed 10 feet above ground, the wood house is the first of its kind in South India — built using Canadian technology and imported materials, and exclusively designed (voluntarily) by a Swedish architect during his stay at Rainforest Munnar Resort.

Our prime objective was to give the occupants an isolated feel in the midst of a forest, while respecting the fawn, flora and every living creature around. The wood house is an exceptional example of the fusion of nature with modern architecture — the picture speaks it all.
