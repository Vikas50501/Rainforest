A Pine-Wood House,
10 Feet Above the Forest

Built on 500 square feet of natural pine wood and placed 10 feet above ground, this wood house is the first of its kind in South India — crafted using Canadian technology and imported materials.

It was exclusively designed by a Swedish architect who stayed at Rainforest Munnar Resort and volunteered his skills. The result is an exceptional fusion of nature and modern architecture — a space that feels alive in the forest.

Naturally restorative

Why a Wooden Home Feels So Good

What Canadians have long understood about living inside natural pine — and what you will feel from your very first breath inside the cottage.

01

No Electrostatic Charge

Natural pine wood does not emit electrostatic charge, creating a measurably calmer and healthier indoor environment from the moment you step inside.

02

Naturally Regulates Humidity

Pine-log walls absorb and release moisture as conditions change — keeping the interior cool in the afternoon and warm through the mountain night.

03

The Wood Breathes

The natural "breathing" property of pine promotes healthy air circulation throughout — fresh, gentle and restorative in a way no concrete wall can replicate.

04

Soothes the Nervous System

The warm grain and amber tones of natural wood positively influence mood and calm the nervous system — exactly what a honeymoon should feel like.

05

Deep, Uninterrupted Sleep

The combined effect of regulated temperature, clean air and zero electromagnetic interference creates ideal conditions for the best rest of your life.

06

Complete Forest Privacy

Elevated 10 feet above the ground and surrounded by forest on all sides, the cottage exists in its own world — undisturbed, intimate and entirely yours.
