A Pine-Wood House,
10 Feet Above the Forest

Built on 500 square feet of natural pine wood and placed 10 feet above ground, this wood house is the first of its kind in South India — crafted using Canadian technology and imported materials.

It was exclusively designed by a Swedish architect who stayed at Rainforest Munnar Resort and volunteered his skills. The result is an exceptional fusion of nature and modern architecture — a space that feels alive in the forest.

Naturally healthy

Why a Wooden Home Feels So Good

What Canadians have long believed about living inside natural pine — and what you will feel from your very first breath.

No Electrostatic Charge

Natural pine wood does not emit electrostatic charge, creating a calmer and healthier indoor environment.

Regulates Humidity

Pine-log walls naturally regulate humidity and temperature, keeping the interior cool in summer and warm in winter.

Breathing Wood

The natural "breathing" property of pine promotes healthy air circulation — fresh, gentle and restorative.

Soothes the Mind

The warm, natural wood colour positively influences the mind and soothes the nervous system — perfect for a honeymoon.

Deep, Restful Sleep

Creates the perfect conditions for recreation and rest — guests consistently report the best sleep of their lives.

Isolated & Private

Surrounded by forest on all sides, elevated above the ground — the cottage gives complete privacy to the newlyweds.
